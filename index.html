Hi,my name is Alex

I'm a self-taught developer at the very beginning of my tech career, I decided to change my career path in 2019 and spend all my time during the COVID lockdown to learn and prepare myself for the developer role by keep learning and contribute to open soucre projects.

In the past 2 years, I have taught myself HTML, CSS, JS, Python, and other popular frameworks & libraries like React/Redux, NEXT.js, Gatsby for the front-end, I also learned backend skills like Node.js, EXPRESS, SQL/NoSQL ...ext.
